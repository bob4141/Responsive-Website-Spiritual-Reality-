About Our Program

Your spirituality is the aspect of you that is tuned into the idea of there being something beyond you. In particular, being spiritual is about recognizing that our human senses don't tell the whole story about the universe. That there is more to us than our bodies and brains. Spirituality might involve endorsing a higher form of life or attributing some kind of conscious awareness to the universe. Spirituality can vary dramatically, but most say that this is what gives their lives a sense of meaning and coherence.
